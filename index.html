About

We are the knowledgehouse, we started the knowledgehouse because the kids in our neighborhood of the South Bronx were jobless. 36% of 16-24 year olds in the South Bronx are disconnected from school and work. At the same time, most technical education programs in the Bronx are delivered in school. By creating local technology programs, in and outside of the classroom, The Knowledge House offers alternative pathways for disconnected young adults, while preventing youth disconnection for high school students..
